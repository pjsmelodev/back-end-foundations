DEDUCTIVE REASONING PROCESS:
    1 - Identify the premises
    2 - Analyze the premises
    3 - Draw conclusions
    4 - Test conclusions

PRACTICE QUIZ:

1. What is deductive reasoning in the context of programming?
    A logical process by which conclusions are drawn from given premises

2. Which is the first step in applying deductive reasoning to solve problems?
    Identifying the premises

3. When solving a problem with deductive reasoning, what should you focus on first?
    Outlining the known premises or assumptions of the problem

4. How does deductive reasoning help in debugging a programming error?
    It helps to systematically identify the root causes by starting from known facts
